## Oracle honesty (applies to all three legs)
The oracle here is **frozen-historical** (the codec's own past output, committed as a `const` with provenance), **not** externally-derived-from-first-principles. That is exactly the eval's prescription and the ONLY oracle type that catches a *symmetric* encode+decode drift. Do NOT over-promise an independent GF/RS/CRC re-derivation (out of scope; T2-b's PGZ decoder was the FEC-correctness analogue, not a wire-layout one). Every frozen `const` carries a provenance comment: **{crate version, git SHA, exact generating call}**.
