- **Caller count / zero-caller-edit feasibility (grep-verified):**

The `cli_repair.rs:99` / `cli_auto_repair.rs:307,352,363,424,425` JSON-based test assertions are UNAFFECTED (they compare against `serde_json::Value`, which stays byte-identical).
